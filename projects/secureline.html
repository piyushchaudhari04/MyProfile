Socio-Connect

1)Friends
2)Posting and Commenting
3)Messaging
4)Likes
5)Adds
This application brings the college students closer very quickly and easily.

Motivation:

1)Now a days student and alumni communication is very important and geting in contact through facebook has become very difficult and also all the students are not benifitted from it.So we need to devise some strategy to overcome this problem in an innovative way.
2)There is need of software which is easy to use and efficient and also makes communication easily.
3)It will bind the pass out sttudents of the college to the college from where-ever they are.
